Our Heritage

Rooted in tradition, crafted for generations

The Meaning of RootHaus

The name RootHaus—Root & Hide Co.—is a synthesis of global luxury and profound cultural significance. 'Root' honors the resilient Iroko tree, a symbol of strength and longevity in West Africa, representing our deep connection to heritage.

'Haus' speaks to the house of craft, where every hide is transformed into an heirloom. Our brand is built on the philosophy of balance and justice, mirroring the celestial alignment and the equilibrium of nature.

The Iroko Tree

Symbol of Strength and Longevity

The Iroko tree stands as one of Africa's most majestic trees, revered across West African cultures for its incredible strength and endurance. Growing up to 50 meters tall and living for hundreds of years, the Iroko embodies the resilience and longevity we aspire to in every piece we create.

Just as the Iroko's roots run deep into the earth, our designs are deeply rooted in Nigerian heritage and traditional craftsmanship. The tree's ability to weather storms and stand strong through generations mirrors our commitment to creating leather goods that become family heirlooms.

Our Values

Heritage

Drawing inspiration from Nigerian artistry and the timeless symbolism of the Iroko tree, our designs are built to connect past and present. Each piece tells a story of cultural pride, traditional craftsmanship, and the enduring spirit of West African heritage. We honor the artisans who came before us while creating pieces that speak to the modern world.

Craft

Only the finest hides, ethically sourced, and meticulously worked by master artisans ensure that every piece is an heirloom, not just an accessory. Our commitment to excellence means every stitch, every cut, and every finish is executed with precision and care. We believe in the slow craft movement—taking the time needed to create something truly exceptional that will last for generations.

Our Process

01

Material Selection

We source only the finest full-grain leather from ethical suppliers, ensuring each hide meets our exacting standards for quality and character.

02

Hand Cutting

Master craftsmen cut each piece by hand, respecting the natural grain and unique characteristics of the leather.

Traditional Assembly

Using time-honored techniques passed down through generations, we assemble each piece with saddle stitching for unmatched durability.

04

Final Finishing

Every edge is burnished, every surface conditioned, and every detail perfected before it bears the RootHaus name.
